Millie Bobby Brown

Biography

Millie Bobby Brown is a famous young British actress. She was born in 2004 in Spain and grew up in the UK.

She became world-famous for acting as Eleven in the popular TV series Stranger Things

She is hard-working and talented, and she also works as a film producer. She is loved by young people all over the world for her lovely look and brave role in many works.

Featured Works

Stranger Things

A hit science-fiction TV series. Millie plays Eleven, a girl with super powers, and this role made her globally famous.

Godzilla: King of the Monsters

A classic monster action movie. She acts as Madison and joins the fight against huge ancient monsters.

Godzilla vs. Kong

Enola Holmes

A popular mystery movie. She plays Enola Holmes, the smart younger sister of Sherlock Holmes, to solve secret cases.

Eleven in Stranger Things
In Stranger Things, Millie Bobby Brown plays Eleven, a quiet girl with superhuman powers who escapes from a secret lab. She joins a group of friends to fight dangerous monsters from the Upside Down and face hidden government secrets. Eleven speaks little in the show, so Millie uses soft facial expressions and eye acting to show fear, loneliness and bravery. She has struggled with hearing problems for years. To act perfectly, she learned all her co‑stars’ lines to keep scenes natural. Thanks to her hard work and wonderful performance, Stranger Things made her world-famous and turned Eleven into a classic role.
